PROJECT: Modern Office Building
ARCHITECT: Design Studio
LOCATION: Urban Core, Mixed-Use District

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:
A contemporary 8-story office building designed to achieve LEED Gold certification. 
The building features a transparent ground floor with retail spaces, and office floors 
above with flexible floor plans. The design emphasizes natural light, energy efficiency, 
and a connection to the urban context.

DESIGN INTENT:
The material palette reflects a modern, sustainable approach to commercial architecture. 
The building should feel light and transparent while maintaining thermal performance. 
Materials should be durable, low-maintenance, and contribute to the building's 
environmental goals.

KEY REQUIREMENTS:
- High-performance glazing systems for energy efficiency
- Durable exterior cladding materials
- Sustainable material sourcing where possible
- Materials suitable for urban environment (durability, maintenance)
- Aesthetic that complements surrounding context
